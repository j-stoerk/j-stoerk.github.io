About

I work at the intersection of machine learning, battery manufacturing, and materials science. My research focuses on continual learning, process understanding, and robust analysis of experimental and electrochemical data for electrode production.

Research Interests

Continual learning

EWC, A-GEM, DER++, replay-based methods, and robust learning under changing process conditions.

Battery electrode manufacturing

Dry electrode production, slurry extrusion, coating, compression, and process parameter optimization.

Electrochemical analytics

EIS analysis, cycling data interpretation, and linking process variables to downstream cell performance.
